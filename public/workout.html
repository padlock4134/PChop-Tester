Form Tips & Workout Advice

3 sets of 15-20 reps | 45 seconds rest between sets

Form Tips:

  • Breathing: Exhale on exertion, inhale on return
  • Core: Keep engaged throughout all exercises
  • Range: Use full range of motion for maximum benefit
  • Tempo: Control the weight, avoid momentum
  • Focus: Maintain mind-muscle connection

Workout Advice:

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes before each session
  • Hydration: Drink water before, during, and after
  • Rest: Allow 48 hours recovery for muscle groups
  • Progression: Increase weight when you can do 12 reps easily
  • Consistency: Follow the program for best results

Sunday Biking + Abs Workouts

🚴‍♂️ Stationary Bike HIIT Workouts (Endurance-Focused)

Bike Workout 1 – Sprint Intervals

  • 5-min warm-up
  • 30 sec sprint (RPE 9–10)
  • 90 sec easy (RPE 3–4)
  • → 6 rounds
  • 5-min cooldown

Bike Workout 2 – Tabata Power

  • 5-min warm-up
  • 20 sec sprint / 10 sec rest
  • → 8x = 1 round
  • → 2–3 total rounds
  • 5-min cooldown

Bike Workout 3 – Climb Intervals

  • 5-min warm-up
  • 1 min high-resistance climb
  • 1 min easy ride
  • → 8–10 rounds
  • 5-min cooldown

Bike Workout 4 – Descending Ladder

  • 5-min warm-up
  • 45 sec sprint / 15 sec rest
  • 40 sec sprint / 20 sec rest
  • ...down to 20 sec
  • → Then climb back up
  • 5-min cooldown

Bike Workout 5 – Mixed Zones

  • 5-min warm-up
  • 1 min fast pace
  • 1 min climb
  • 30 sec sprint
  • 1 min easy
  • → 4–5 rounds
  • 5-min cooldown

Rest Day

Recovery Day

Rest days are crucial for muscle recovery and growth. Use this time to:

  • Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours)
  • Stay hydrated
  • Maintain proper nutrition
  • Consider light stretching or walking
  • Foam rolling for muscle recovery

Remember: Rest days are when your muscles actually grow and repair!
