# PorkChop Ed Tech - 100 Target Investors (Boston/NYC/Chicago/Philly)

## Investment Thesis
PorkChop is a B2B EdTech platform for hands-on skills training, using culinary education as initial beachhead market. Our video-based assessment and AI-powered feedback system addresses the $2.1B culinary education gap while expanding to broader vocational training markets (healthcare labs, trade skills, scientific research). Purpose-built platform with video portfolios, AI-powered skill assessment, live training sessions, and comprehensive administration tools. B2B SaaS model targeting community colleges, mid-market universities, and premier culinary schools.

**Company Status:**
- **LLC Filed:** Corporate structure established, ready for investment
- **Utility Patent Pending:** IP protection for video-based skill assessment system

**Target Market Segments:**
- **Community College Systems:** Large-scale vocational programs, workforce development
- **Mid-Market Universities:** Culinary programs without extensive bench science degrees
- **Premier Culinary Schools:** Whales like Johnson & Wales, Culinary Institute of America

**Market Expansion Strategy:**
- Phase 1: Culinary education (600+ US schools, $2.1B market)
- Phase 2: Healthcare skills training (nursing labs, medical simulation)
- Phase 3: Trade and technical skills (welding, automotive, manufacturing)
- Phase 4: Scientific research training (lab techniques, protocols)

## Founder Story & Mission

**Personal Journey:**
Survived childhood homelessness and physical abuse, learned to grind and develop skills to survive. Took abuse to protect younger brother who later struggled with heroin addiction but recovered. Forced into child labor (splitting wood, shoveling snow) while adults slept in comfort. Worked exhaustingly through high school - fell asleep in classes from working until 10pm without a driver's license.

**The Kitchen Connection:**
Grandfather pulled me into the kitchen as sanctuary - taught me that skills create dignity and opportunity. Culinary training became the path from survival to thriving. This lived experience drives our mission to make hands-on skills training accessible to all students, especially those from disadvantaged backgrounds.

**Why This Matters:**
- **First-generation students** need practical pathways to economic mobility
- **Survival skills** translate to workforce readiness  
- **Hands-on training** builds confidence where traditional education fails
- **Video assessment** creates opportunity for students who can't access traditional apprenticeships

**Mission Alignment:**
Every feature - video portfolios, AI feedback, live sessions - designed to help students like me use hands-on skills to change their life trajectory.
