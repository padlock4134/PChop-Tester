## PorkChop Platform Reference (applies to all partners)

PorkChop is a **white-label LMS for trade schools**. Organizations upload their own curriculum; AI populates discipline modules. Content-agnostic by design.

**Disciplines:** Electrical, HVAC, Plumbing, Construction, Welding, Manufacturing, Automotive, Logistics, Culinary + custom  
**Core Modules (per discipline):**
- **Workspace** — Hands-on practice hub (uploads, scans, job tickets)
- **Notebook** — Save and organize discipline-specific content
- **Community** — Social timeline, group finder, live sessions
- **School** — Syllabus viewer, lesson progress, bench practice timers
- **AI Assistant** — Contextual curriculum assistant, unique persona per discipline
- **Admin Dashboard** — School branding, curriculum upload, gradebook, analytics

**Credential/Outcome Tracking:**
- Gradebook with rubric-based assessment and instructor feedback
- Video portfolio submission and review
- Skills Wallet — exports credentials to Credivera, IQ4, Velocity, LearnCard, or state systems

**RFP Question Mapping (shared across all proposals):**
- **Q7 (Catalytic Impact):** PorkChop is the named technology platform asset
- **Q12 (Organizational Capacity):** PorkChop is the named technology partner with defined role
- **Q14 (Data Collection Methodology):** PorkChop's gradebook + analytics is the data collection plan
- **Biannual KPI Reporting:** PorkChop tracks enrollment, credential attainment, retention, and wage progression at the participant level

**Budget Line:** "Technology + Software" — PorkChop is priced at **$49 per user, per month**. At 300 active users that is **$176,400/year ($352,800 over two years)**; rounded to the grant budget as **$175,000/year ($350,000 over two years)**. PorkChop is roughly **36% of the total award**, which is appropriate for a capacity-building proposal where the technology platform is the central intervention.
