# PorkChop Ed Tech
## The First Purpose-Built LMS for Culinary Education

---

## The Problem

**Nursing students get vSim. Engineering students get CAD. Culinary students get crosswords.**

Trade schools—starting with culinary—lack discipline-specific learning management systems. While generic LMS platforms dominate the market, culinary students are stuck with:
- Generic LMS platforms (Blackboard, Canvas) that don't understand their domain
- Paper-based skill assessments with no video submission capability
- Zero tools for ingredient management or kitchen simulation
- No way to practice technique outside the physical classroom

**Market Reality:** 500+ US institutions offer culinary/hospitality programs, with 1,000+ community colleges serving trade disciplines—all underserved by legacy LMS platforms built for lecture halls, not kitchens or garages. (Source: IPEDS 2023, AACC 2024)

---

## The Solution

**PorkChop** is a content-agnostic, AI-powered LMS platform. 

**Schools provide their own curriculum—we deploy it automatically.**

Starting with culinary as our wedge market, but the architecture works for **any trade discipline**: nursing, automotive, cosmetology, welding, HVAC.

### How It Works:

1. **School uploads their curriculum** (PDFs, DOCX, syllabi)
2. **AI analyzes and auto-maps content** to platform modules with confidence scoring
3. **Platform deploys** with school's branding and structure in days, not months
4. **No vendor curriculum lock-in** — schools own their content, we provide the infrastructure

---

## Platform Differentiators

**What makes PorkChop different from legacy platforms like Blackboard/Canvas:**

### 1. AI Content Processor (Patent-Protected)
- Schools upload curriculum files → AI extracts, analyzes, and auto-maps to modules
- Generates metadata (week numbers, topics, equipment, difficulty levels)
- Admin reviews AI suggestions with confidence scores before publishing

- **Result:** Deploy a fully-populated LMS in days instead of months of manual course building

### 2. Video Submission with Rubric Grading
- Students record cooking technique videos directly in-app
- Instructors grade with customizable rubrics (knife skills, plating, safety, etc.)
- Assignment management with points, weights, due dates
- Video library with category filters for practice review

- **Result:** Assess actual hands-on skills, not just written tests

### 3. AI Ingredient Scanner + Recipe Matcher
- Computer vision scans pantry items, auto-categorizes into 10 kitchen categories
- Fuzzy matching algorithm scores recipes by available ingredients
- Adapts to student's kitchen setup (Dorm Life → Full Chef's Kitchen)
- Filters by dietary preferences and cuisine types

- **Result:** Students practice with what they actually have, reducing barriers to homework completion

### 4. Gamification Engine
- WoW Classic-inspired 60-level XP progression (Dishwasher → Toji Master)
- 160+ badges across 7 categories (Weekly Challenge, Milestone, Difficulty, Theme, Streak, Community, Special)
- Talent trees with equipment unlocks (Cast Iron Champion, Grilling Heavy Weight, Baking Warlock)
- 52 weekly rotating challenges with XP rewards

- **Result:** Student engagement vs. compliance-focused legacy LMS

### Additional Capabilities:
- **Global Test Kitchen** — Live streaming with session recording for remote/hybrid learning
- **Chef Freddie AI Assistant** — Context-aware help, recipe generation, curriculum creation
- **AR Practice Mode** — Camera-based hand tracking for technique practice outside physical labs
- **Market Directory** — Location-based local market finder to support ingredient sourcing
- **White-Label Ready** — Full branding customization per school
- **Bilingual** — English and Spanish (i18n ready for additional languages)
