# PorkChop Go-To-Market Strategy

## 🎯 Four-Channel Growth Strategy

| Channel | Owner | Target | Deal Size | Timeline | Purpose |
|---------|-------|--------|-----------|----------|---------|
| **1. State Systems** | Founder + Sales Pro | 10-15 systems | $100K-500K | 12-18mo | Volume & Scale |
| **2. Individual Colleges** | New Hire | 50-100 schools | $10K-50K | 3-6mo | Proof Points |
| **3. Whale Institutes** | Founder | CIA, JWU, LCB, ICE | $500K-1M+ | 18-24mo | Brand Validation |
| **4. DTC/Platform** | Future Hire (Mo 6-12) | Individual users | $10-30/mo | Immediate | User Data & Distribution |

---

## 📈 How Channels Feed Each Other

```
Individual Colleges → Create proof points
    ↓
State Systems → Reference individual success
    ↓
Whale Institutes → Validate entire category
    ↓
Trade Press → "CIA Adopts PorkChop"
    ↓
All B2B Channels → Close faster with whale validation
    ↓
DTC Channel → Bottom-up demand (students/faculty ask schools)
    ↓
User Data → Informs B2B product roadmap
```

**Key Insight:** One whale win (CIA) accelerates everything else. State systems feel safe following CIA's lead. Individual colleges close 2x faster with whale logos.

## 🏢 CHANNEL 1: State Community College Systems

### Owner
**Founder + Sales Pro**

### Target List (Priority)
- California Community Colleges (116 schools, ~5K students) → $250K-375K/year
- SUNY New York (64 schools, ~3.5K students) → $175K-260K/year
- Texas Community Colleges (50 schools, ~2.5K students) → $125K-190K/year
- Florida College System (28 schools, ~2K students) → $100K-150K/year

### Sales Process (12-18 months)
1. **Research (Mo 1-2):** Decision makers, budget cycles, current LMS pain points
2. **Warm Intro (Mo 3-4):** Leverage sales pro network, reference individual colleges in system
3. **Discovery (Mo 5-6):** Problem validation, understand priorities, political landscape
4. **Pilot Proposal (Mo 7-9):** 3-5 campus pilot, 90-120 days, $25-50K funded by system
5. **Pilot Execution (Mo 10-12):** Weekly check-ins, gather metrics, testimonials, case study
6. **System Proposal (Mo 13-15):** Present pilot results, ROI analysis, system-wide pricing
7. **Contract (Mo 16-18):** Navigate procurement/RFP, negotiate, close multi-year deal

### Division of Labor
**Sales Pro:** Relationships, procurement navigation, RFP response, contract negotiations  
**Founder:** Vision pitch, technical demos, pilot design, product roadmap, implementation planning

### Pricing
- **Pilot:** $25-50K for 3-5 campuses, 90-120 days
- **System-Wide:** $50-75/student/year (volume discount)
- **Example:** 2,350 students × $60 = $141K/year

### Key Message
> "Give your 47 culinary programs the same tools nursing already has. One platform. One contract. System-wide outcomes."

## 🎓 CHANNEL 2: Individual Colleges

### Owner
**New Hire (Seller)**

### Target Profile
- Mid-market universities, technical colleges
- 100-500 culinary students per program
- $10K-50K available at department level
- Progressive program directors seeking competitive edge

### Sales Process (3-6 months)
1. **Week 1-2:** 50-75 cold calls, 10-15 discovery calls scheduled
2. **Week 3-4:** 5-7 demo meetings from discovery calls
3. **Week 5-6:** 2-3 pilot commitments (30-day free trial, one class)
4. **Week 7-10:** Pilots running, weekly check-ins, gather testimonials
5. **Week 11-12:** Present results, propose full deployment, close deal

### New Hire Metrics
**Month 1:** 2 pilot commitments  
**Month 2:** 4 pilots running, 1 paying customer  
**Month 3:** 6 pilots, 2 paying customers  
**Month 6:** 10 paying customers ($200K ARR)

### Compensation
- Base: $50-60K
- Commission: $500-1K per pilot, $5-10K per conversion
- Bonus: $1K per testimonial

### Pricing
- **Pilot:** Free 30 days, one class
- **Full Program:** $100-150/student/year
- **Example:** 200 students × $125 = $25K/year

### Key Message
> "Your students deserve better than PDFs. Start building video portfolios this semester. 30-day free pilot, no contract."

### Sales Process (18-24 months)
1. **Build Case (Mo 1-6):** Run pilots at individual colleges, gather metrics, get trade press coverage
2. **Warm Intro (Mo 7-12):** Alumni network, advisory board, industry conferences
3. **Vision Pitch (Mo 13):** 60-min meeting with President/Provost
4. **Pilot (Mo 13-18):** ONE campus, 90 days, defined metrics
5. **System Proposal (Mo 19-21):** ROI analysis, all-campus rollout
6. **Contract (Mo 22-24):** Multi-year deal, press release, phased deployment

### 60-Minute Vision Pitch Structure
1. **Opening (5 min):** Homework gap - nursing $10K vs culinary $0
2. **Problem Validation (10 min):** Their specific challenges (research-backed)
3. **Solution Demo (15 min):** Platform features that matter to THEM
4. **Strategic Partnership (15 min):** Co-development, co-branding, first-mover advantage
5. **Pilot Proposal (10 min):** Low-risk start, one campus, defined success metrics
6. **Next Steps (5 min):** Faculty meeting, IT review, pilot agreement

## 🛒 CHANNEL 4: DTC/Platform Integration

### Owner
**Future Hire (Month 6-12)**

### Target Partners
- **Meal Kits:** HelloFresh, Blue Apron, Home Chef
- **Grocery:** Instacart, Thrive Market, Whole Foods
- **Equipment:** Williams Sonoma, Sur La Table
- **Recipe Platforms:** Tasty, AllRecipes, Food Network Kitchen

### The Pitch to Partners
> "Your customers buy ingredients/equipment. PorkChop helps them USE it. We increase your retention by making cooking easier."

### Integration Models
1. **Embedded Trial:** "30 days free PorkChop with your HelloFresh box"
2. **Co-Branded:** "HelloFresh Recipe Matcher powered by PorkChop"
3. **API Integration:** Auto-sync ingredients from partner to PorkChop
4. **Revenue Share:** 70/30 or 60/40 split on subscriptions

### Timeline
- **Mo 1-3:** Consumer MVP (simplified onboarding, core features only)
- **Mo 4-6:** First platform partner pilot (1K trials, 90 days)
- **Mo 7-12:** Optimize conversion, add 2-3 partners, reach 5-10K users
- **Year 2:** 5-7 partners, 25K+ users, $500K MRR

### Fourth Hire Profile
**Title:** Head of Consumer Growth / DTC Partnerships  
**Timing:** Month 6-12 after B2B provides cash flow  
**Skills:** Platform partnerships, growth marketing, API integration, consumer product  
**Comp:** $80-120K base + 0.5-1% equity + revenue share  
**Metrics:** Mo 3 = first partner, Mo 6 = 1K users, Mo 12 = 5K users

### Strategic Value
**User Data → B2B Product:** Consumer insights inform school features  
**Demand → B2B Sales:** "10K users" validates product for schools  
**Platform Partners → Distribution:** HelloFresh 7M users = massive reach
