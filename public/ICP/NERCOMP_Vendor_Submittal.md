## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

PorkChop Ed Tech is a purpose-built Learning Management System (LMS) designed for practical trades and arts education programs. While we're launching with culinary education as our wedge market, our platform serves any hands-on discipline that requires portfolio-based assessment, technique demonstration, and specialized learning tools.

We address the "homework gap" in trade education—while nursing students receive sophisticated simulation software ($10K+ value) and engineering students access professional CAD tools, trade students have been limited to generic LMS platforms and crossword puzzles.

PorkChop provides trade and arts programs with:
- **Digital portfolio management** with AI-powered content analysis
- **Video technique grading** system with discipline-specific rubrics
- **Live demonstration capabilities** for real-time instruction
- **Complete LMS replacement** with trade-specific features
- **Flexible curriculum framework** adaptable to any practical discipline
