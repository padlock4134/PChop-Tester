# Co-Founding CTO - The Cucina (PorkChop Ed Tech)

## About The Opportunity

We're seeking a **Co-Founding CTO** to join The Cucina, an innovative B2B ed-tech platform transforming culinary education. This is an **equity-based partnership** for someone who wants to build something meaningful from the ground up.

## What We've Built

- **Legal Entity**: Established LLC with proper corporate structure
- **IP Protection**: Utility patent secured
- **Market Traction**: Growing from 3 to 12+ culinary schools in recent weeks
- **Product**: Live platform with institutional features including gradebook integration, student progress tracking, and social learning modules
- **Business Model**: B2B institutional partnerships with culinary schools
- **Pipeline**: Active pilot programs and expanding school partnerships

## The Mission

We're closing the gap between culinary education and traditional academic disciplines. Culinary students deserve the same digital learning tools, assessment systems, and institutional support as students in any other field.

## What We Need

A technical co-founder who can:

- **Scale the platform** to support rapid institutional growth
- **Architect for education** with understanding of LMS integration, data privacy (FERPA), and institutional requirements
- **Build for reliability** as schools depend on our platform for daily instruction
- **Lead technical strategy** as we expand from culinary to other vocational education verticals
- **Own the technology** with full autonomy over technical decisions and architecture

## What You'll Get

- **Significant equity stake** - this is a co-founder position, not a hire
- **Full technical ownership** - you'll build and lead the engineering vision
- **Real impact** - affecting thousands of students and educators

- **Proven traction** - you're joining a moving train, not starting from zero

- **Startup experience** - you've built products from scratch before
- **Education tech passion** - you care about democratizing education
- **Full-stack capabilities** - comfortable across the entire technical stack
- **Institutional understanding** - bonus if you understand school systems, compliance, and B2B sales cycles
