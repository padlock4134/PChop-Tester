## Slide 2 — Problem
Generic LMS platforms were built for lecture-based learning, not hands-on competency.

- Instructors lack native workflows for practical skill assessment
- Students cannot easily demonstrate technique progression outside the lab
- Program teams spend too much time manually configuring curriculum in legacy systems
- Institutions need accessible delivery that does not depend on premium hardware

**Bottom line:** Trade programs are forced into tools that do not match how skills are actually taught.

---

## Slide 3 — Why Now
- Workforce pathways and trade education demand are growing
- Institutions need hybrid-ready practical assessment workflows
- AI + vision tools are now production-capable for curriculum ops and skills workflows
- Schools are under pressure to improve outcomes while controlling deployment overhead

---

## Slide 4 — Solution
PorkChop is a vertical LMS built for hands-on programs.

### Core platform pillars
1. **AI Curriculum Processor**  
   Upload school curriculum (PDF/DOCX/TXT) → AI-assisted module mapping with admin review.
2. **Video Skill Assessment**  
   Native student submissions + rubric-based instructor grading.
3. **Practice & Engagement Layer**  
   Scanner/matching workflows, challenges, and progression tools that support practice outside class.
4. **PWA Access by Design**  
   Cross-device access without requiring expensive iOS/VR hardware.
