# PorkChop — Complete App Recap
**The First Purpose-Built LMS for Culinary Education**

*Last Updated: March 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

PorkChop is a vertical SaaS Learning Management System built specifically for culinary education institutions. It replaces generic platforms (Canvas, Blackboard) that were designed for lecture halls — not kitchens. The platform combines AI-powered tools, hands-on skill assessment, gamification, and entrepreneurship training into a single product purpose-built for trade school workflows.

The platform is content-agnostic: schools upload their own curriculum, and PorkChop's AI auto-maps it to modules. PorkChop is starting with culinary as the wedge market, with the infrastructure designed to expand into other trade disciplines (nursing, automotive, cosmetology, welding, HVAC) as clients are ready.

PorkChop is a Maine based LLC with a utility patent pending.

---

## The Problem

- **500+ US institutions** offer culinary/hospitality programs; **1,000+ community colleges** serve trade disciplines — all underserved by legacy LMS platforms.
- Nursing students get vSim simulators. Engineering students get CAD software. **Culinary students get crossword puzzles.**
- Generic LMS platforms have no concept of ingredient management, kitchen simulation, technique assessment, or practical skills grading.
- Paper-based skill assessments, no video submission capability, and zero tools for practicing technique outside the physical classroom.

---

## The Solution

Schools provide their own curriculum — PorkChop deploys it automatically.

1. **School uploads curriculum** (PDFs, DOCX, syllabi)
2. **AI analyzes and auto-maps content** to platform modules with confidence scoring
3. **Platform deploys** with the school's branding and structure in days, not months
4. **No vendor curriculum lock-in** — schools own their content, PorkChop provides infrastructure

## Core Modules

### 1. My Kitchen — Ingredient Scanning & Recipe Matching

Students photograph their fridge or pantry. Computer vision identifies ingredients. AI generates recipes matched to what they actually have.

- **AI-powered ingredient scanning** — snap a photo, get a categorized ingredient list
- **Digital Cupboard** — persistent ingredient inventory organized into 10 categories
- **Intelligent Recipe Matching** — AI generates recipes adapted to the student's skill level, kitchen setup, and available equipment
- **Skill-level adaptation** — recipes adjust for beginners (simple, fast) through advanced cooks (complex techniques)
- **Kitchen Setup awareness** — considers equipment ranging from "Dorm Life" to "Full Chef's Kitchen"
- **Talent Trees** — specialty paths (e.g., Cast Iron, Grilling, Baking) that personalize the experience
- **Health & Nutrition tags** — dietary classifications and real nutritional data for every recipe

### 2. My CookBook — Recipe Collection & Academic Gradebook

A digital recipe portfolio that doubles as a grading system for culinary programs.

- **Recipe management** — save, organize, search, and filter recipes
- **Interactive cookbook UI** — skeuomorphic page-turning interface
- **Recipe collections & sharing** — themed groupings and social sharing
- **Video submission system** — students record cooking technique videos directly in-app
- **Assignment management** — weekly assignments with points, weights, and due dates
- **Gradebook** — instructor grading with customizable rubrics (knife skills, plating, safety, etc.)
- **Video library** — categorized video archive for practice review

### 3. Culinary School — Curriculum & Practice

Structured learning with a full year of weekly techniques, tutorials, nutrition analysis, and hands-on practice.

- **52 Weekly Techniques** — one fundamental cooking technique per week, organized across four skill domains: Knife & Prep, Heat & Temperature, Flavor Building, and Texture & Technique
- **Automatic tutorial sourcing** — matched video content for each recipe and technique
- **Per-recipe nutrition breakdown** — real USDA nutritional analysis
- **Built-in cooking timer** — for timed practice sessions
- **Syllabus integration** — structured course display tied to school curriculum
- **Bench Practice (AR)** — interactive practice scenes with guided overlays, technique tracking, and step-by-step feedback

### 4. Chef's Corner — Community & Entrepreneurship

Social hub where students connect, showcase dishes, find local markets, and build menus — real-world business skills, not just cooking.

- **Global Test Kitchen** — live cooking sessions with streaming, scheduling, recording, and classroom integration
- **Market Directory** — discover nearby grocery stores, fish markets, farmers markets, and specialty shops with ingredient-to-market mapping
- **Build Menu** — create menus from saved recipes
- **Recipe Showcase** — feature dishes with full nutrition breakdown and serving adjustments

## Cross-Cutting Features

### Chef Freddie — AI Assistant
A contextual AI chef assistant available on every page. Helps with recipe suggestions, cooking tips, equipment advice, and general culinary questions. Conversations are tracked for institutional analytics.

### Gamification System
- **60-level progression** inspired by RPG leveling systems
- **Level titles** follow a real kitchen hierarchy: Dishwasher → Prep Cook → Line Cook → Sous Chef → Executive Chef → Master Chef → Legendary Chef (and many more in between)
- **XP rewards** for meaningful actions: daily logins, recipe completions, lesson progress, challenge completions, and more
- **120+ badges** across 7 categories: Weekly Challenge, Milestone, Difficulty, Theme, Streak, Community, and Special
- **52 Weekly Challenges** — rotating themed challenges with XP rewards and unique badges

### Admin Dashboard (Institutional)
A comprehensive administration panel for school administrators and instructors:

- **User management** — student, faculty, and alumni directories
- **Analytics** — activity tracking, program performance, enrollment health, content analytics
- **AI Curriculum Processor** — upload syllabi and course documents; AI extracts and maps content to platform modules with confidence scoring for admin review
- **Branding & configuration** — school-specific customization and module toggling
- **Job placement tracking** — post-graduation employment data
- **Data export** — institutional reporting tools

### Student Progress Dashboard
- Curriculum progress tracking
- Skills assessment overview
- Engagement metrics
- Live session discovery

### Profile & Preferences
- Editable profile with dietary, cuisine, kitchen setup, and experience level preferences
- XP display with level title
- AI-generated progress reports exportable to PDF
- Fully responsive across all device types
