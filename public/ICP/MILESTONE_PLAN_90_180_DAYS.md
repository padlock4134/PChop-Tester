# PorkChop Milestone Plan (90–180 Days)

## Objective
Convert current pipeline signal into repeatable paid institutional adoption while building a scalable onboarding and delivery engine.

---

## Snapshot (Starting Point)
- Product is built and demoable across core instructor/student/admin workflows
- 4–5 pilot opportunities in motion
- 4–5 institutional/strategic partnerships in motion
- Raise target: $350K pre-seed SAFE

---

## 90-Day Milestones (Execution + Conversion)

### 1) Pilot Conversion Pipeline Discipline
**Goal:** Move active opportunities into formal pilot agreements with clear success criteria.

- Standardize pilot qualification checklist (buyer fit, timeline, implementation owner, budget path)
- Define stage-based pipeline process with weekly conversion reviews
- Convert qualified opportunities into signed pilot plans

**Success signals:**
- Pilot funnel has documented stage progression and owners
- Signed pilot plans with explicit outcomes and evaluation windows

### 2) Onboarding Velocity Foundation
**Goal:** Reduce time from agreement to first live instructional use.

- Build institution onboarding playbook (technical, training, launch checklist)
- Package curriculum intake workflow with admin review gates
- Launch baseline instructor enablement materials (quick-start + grading workflow)

**Success signals:**
- Faster implementation handoff to first live usage
- Repeatable onboarding steps used across multiple institutions

### 3) Product Readiness for Institutional Delivery
**Goal:** Prioritize reliability and implementation-critical features.

- Focus roadmap on pilot blockers (not feature sprawl)
- Harden operational tooling for support, issue triage, and usage visibility
- Tighten compliance/security communication artifacts for buyer confidence

**Success signals:**
- Reduced implementation friction during pilot launch
- Clear operator dashboard for customer health and usage behavior

### 4) Maine Hiring Plan (Initial Build)
**Goal:** Add targeted local capacity to support conversion and delivery.

- Define first local hires (implementation/GTM support and operational support)
- Build role scorecards tied directly to pilot success metrics
- Start candidate pipeline and interview cadence

**Success signals:**
- Priority roles opened and actively in funnel
- Hiring tied to near-term customer and onboarding outcomes

---

## 180-Day Milestones (Repeatability + Revenue Proof)

### 1) Pilot-to-Paid Conversion Engine
**Goal:** Demonstrate repeatable commercial motion.

- Convert successful pilots into paid institutional contracts
- Document conversion triggers and objections for consistent playbook use
- Build referenceable case studies and proof artifacts

**Success signals:**
- Paid customer conversion from pilot cohort
- Evidence of repeatable conversion path across institutions

### 2) Customer Goal: 10–15 Institutional Accounts by End of 2026
**Goal:** Establish durable revenue base and clear growth signal.

- Prioritize highest-probability ICP segments
- Sequence outreach based on implementation capacity and conversion likelihood
- Maintain disciplined expansion from pilot wins to program-level adoption

**Success signals:**
- Account growth tracking toward 10–15-customer end-of-2026 objective
- Improved close velocity and stronger expansion opportunities

### 3) Onboarding Systemization + Reduced Vendor Dependence
**Goal:** Productize deployment so implementation is less vendor-dependent.

- Convert onboarding know-how into standardized workflows and assets
- Expand self-serve components for institution-side setup where appropriate
- Reduce manual intervention requirements over time

**Success signals:**
- More consistent time-to-value across new accounts
- Lower delivery burden per new implementation

### 4) Team Buildout in Maine (Execution Capacity)
**Goal:** Build local execution team aligned to growth and customer success.

- Fill initial local roles and onboard to operating cadence
- Tie hiring performance to conversion and retention outcomes
- Maintain lean, milestone-driven burn discipline

**Success signals:**
- Functional local support capacity in place
- Hiring impact visible in onboarding speed and customer outcomes

---

## Metrics Dashboard (Investor Update Format)
Track and report monthly:

- Pilot stages by institution (qualified, proposed, signed, live, completed)
- Pilot-to-paid conversion rate
- Time from agreement to first live instructional use
- Active usage indicators (instructor and student activity)
- Paid accounts progress toward 10–15 customer target
- Hiring progress against defined Maine role scorecards

---

## Capital Use Alignment
Milestones are funded against four priorities:

1. Pilot delivery quality and implementation success
2. GTM conversion execution
3. Core operational runway (tech stack, infrastructure, compliance, admin)
4. Founder full-time execution continuity + selective Maine hiring

---

## Operating Principle
Signal-driven execution: ship, measure, tighten, repeat.
