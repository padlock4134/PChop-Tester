# STATE COMMUNITY COLLEGE SYSTEMS

## Overview
This document lists state-level community college systems across the United States. These are centralized governing bodies that oversee multiple community colleges within a state, making them ideal targets for system-wide PorkChop adoption.

## SALES STRATEGY

### **System-Wide Approach**
1. **Target central administration first**
   - Present PorkChop as system-wide solution
   - Negotiate volume pricing for entire system
   - Standardize culinary education across all campuses

2. **Pilot Program**
   - Start with 1-2 flagship campuses
   - Demonstrate ROI and student outcomes
   - Expand to entire system

3. **Key Decision Makers**
   - System Chancellor/President
   - VP of Academic Affairs
   - Culinary Program Directors (system-wide)
   - IT/Technology Directors

### **Value Proposition for Systems**
- **Standardization**: Same platform across all campuses
- **Cost Savings**: Volume pricing vs. individual campus purchases
- **Data Analytics**: System-wide reporting and insights
- **Shared Resources**: Recipe libraries, curriculum, best practices
- **Brand Consistency**: Unified culinary education experience
