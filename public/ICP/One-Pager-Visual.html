The first purpose-built learning platform for kitchens, garages and benches!

The Problem

Nursing students get vSim. Engineering students get CAD. Bench students get crosswords.

Culinary programs lack discipline-specific learning platforms. While nursing programs invest $10K+ per student in simulation software, culinary students are stuck with:

  • Generic platforms (Blackboard, Canvas) that don't understand their domain
  • Paper-based skill assessments with no video submission capability
  • Zero tools for ingredient management or kitchen simulation
  • No way to practice technique outside the physical classroom

Market Reality: 500+ US institutions offer culinary/hospitality programs, with 1,000+ community colleges serving trade disciplines—all underserved by legacy platforms built for lecture halls, not kitchens or garages. (Source: IPEDS 2023, AACC 2024)

The Solution

PorkChop is a content-agnostic, AI-powered learning platform.
Starting with culinary, expanding to automotive, welding, HVAC, and cosmetology.

How It Works:

  1. School uploads their curriculum (PDFs, DOCX, syllabi)
  2. AI analyzes and auto-maps content to platform modules with confidence scoring
  3. Platform deploys with school's branding and structure in days, not months
  4. No vendor curriculum lock-in — schools own their content, we provide the infrastructure

Platform Differentiators

What makes PorkChop different from legacy platforms like Blackboard/Canvas:

1. AI Content Processor (Patent-Protected)

  • Schools upload curriculum files → AI extracts, analyzes, and auto-maps to modules
  • Generates metadata (week numbers, topics, equipment, difficulty levels)
  • Admin reviews AI suggestions with confidence scores before publishing

→ Deploy a fully-populated platform in days instead of months of manual course building

2. Video Submission with Rubric Grading

  • Students record cooking technique videos directly in-app
  • Instructors grade with customizable rubrics (knife skills, plating, safety, etc.)
  • Assignment management with points, weights, due dates

→ Assess actual hands-on skills, not just written tests

3. AI Ingredient Scanner + Recipe Matcher

  • Computer vision scans pantry items, auto-categorizes into 10 kitchen categories
  • Fuzzy matching algorithm scores recipes by available ingredients
  • Adapts to student's kitchen setup (Dorm Life → Full Chef's Kitchen)

→ Students practice with what they actually have, reducing barriers to homework completion

4. Gamification Engine

  • WoW Classic-inspired 60-level XP progression (Dishwasher → Toji Master)
  • 160+ badges across 7 categories
  • Talent trees with equipment unlocks

→ Student engagement vs. compliance-focused legacy platforms

Additional Capabilities:

  • Global Test Kitchen — Live streaming with session recording
  • Chef Freddie AI Assistant — Context-aware help, recipe generation
  • AR Practice Mode — Camera-based hand tracking for technique practice
  • White-Label Ready — Full branding customization per school
