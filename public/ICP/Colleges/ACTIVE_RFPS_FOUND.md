## 💡 THE TRUTH ABOUT COMMUNITY COLLEGE RFPS

### Why There Are No Active RFPs Right Now:

1. **Timing** - Most ed-tech RFPs happen in spring/summer for fall implementation
2. **Budget Cycles** - Community colleges finalize budgets in spring
3. **Procurement Process** - Takes 3-6 months from need identification to RFP
4. **Relationship-Based** - 80% of ed-tech deals happen through direct sales, not RFPs
