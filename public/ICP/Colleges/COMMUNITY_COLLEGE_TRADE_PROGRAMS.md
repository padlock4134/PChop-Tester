# Community College Trade Programs - The Cucina Platform Expansion

**Platform Value Proposition**: Digital infrastructure for video-based competency assessment and progress tracking in hands-on trade programs.

**What We Provide**: Video submission system, gradebook integration, progress dashboards, institutional reporting, LMS sync - NOT curriculum.

**What Schools Keep**: Their existing curriculum, textbooks, lesson plans, and certification standards.

---

## Target Market Overview

- **1,050+ Community Colleges** across the United States
- **Focus**: Skilled trade programs requiring hands-on competency demonstration
- **Exclusions**: Medical/healthcare programs (HIPAA complexity), cosmetology (excluded per request)

---

## Tier 1 - Highest Priority Trade Programs

### **1. Welding Technology**
**Market Size**: 800+ community colleges nationwide

**Why It Fits:**
- Schools teach AWS (American Welding Society) standards - existing curriculum
- Students must demonstrate weld quality through video submissions
- Instructors grade bead appearance, penetration, joint consistency
- Certification tracking toward AWS certifications

**Platform Features:**
- **Video Submissions**: Students film weld beads, joint completion, safety procedures
- **Equipment Tracking**: MIG/TIG/Stick welders, grinders, plasma cutters, PPE
- **Gradebook Integration**: Competency-based progression toward AWS certification
- **Rubric Grading**: Instructors assess technique, safety, quality asynchronously
- **Progress Dashboard**: Visual tracking of welding competencies

**Certification Bodies**: AWS (American Welding Society)

### **3. Commercial Truck Driving (CDL Programs)**
**Market Size**: 500+ community colleges nationwide

**Why It Fits:**
- **Massive driver shortage** - schools expanding CDL programs rapidly
- **FMCSA-mandated training hours** require documented proof
- Pre-trip inspections have 100+ checkpoints that must be demonstrated
- Backing maneuvers need repeated practice and assessment
- Video practice for state CDL road tests

**Platform Features:**
- **Video Submissions**: Pre-trip inspections (engine, air brakes, coupling), backing maneuvers (parallel park, alley dock, offset), road skills
- **Equipment Tracking**: Class A trucks, trailers, cones, backing range equipment
- **Gradebook Integration**: FMCSA-compliant skills tracking
- **Rubric Grading**: Instructors assess inspection thoroughness, backing accuracy, safety compliance
- **Progress Dashboard**: Track readiness for CDL Class A/B road test
- **Endorsement Tracking**: Hazmat, Tanker, Doubles/Triples endorsements

**Certification Bodies**: FMCSA (Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration), State DMV CDL programs

**Special Opportunity**: Remote instruction - instructors can review student practice videos outside of limited truck time
