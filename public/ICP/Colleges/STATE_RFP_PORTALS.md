## National Aggregator Sites (Monitor All States)

### BidNet Direct
**URL:** https://www.bidnet.com/
**Cost:** Paid subscription
**Coverage:** Nationwide education RFPs
**Notes:** Best for tracking multiple states

### DemandStar
**URL:** https://www.demandstar.com/
**Cost:** Paid subscription
**Coverage:** Government and education bids nationwide

### GovWin IQ (Deltek)
**URL:** https://www.deltek.com/en/products/govwin-iq
**Cost:** Paid subscription (expensive)
**Coverage:** Federal, state, local, education

### RFP SchoolWatch
**URL:** https://www.rfpschoolwatch.com/
**Cost:** Paid subscription
**Coverage:** Education sector specific
**Notes:** **RECOMMENDED** for ed-tech companies

### NIGP (National Institute of Governmental Purchasing)
**URL:** https://www.nigp.org/
**Cost:** Membership required
**Coverage:** Public procurement nationwide
