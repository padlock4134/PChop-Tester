## 🎨 DESIGN NOTES FOR CANVA

### **Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Your brand blue (#[Maine Blue from app])
- **Accent:** Seafoam green (#[Seafoam from app])
- **Alert/CTA:** Lobster red (#[Lobster Red from app])
- **Neutral:** Weathered white/Sand tones
- **Text:** Dark gray or charcoal (not pure black)

### **Typography:**
- **Headlines:** Bold, sans-serif (Montserrat Bold or similar)
- **Subheadings:** Semi-bold, sans-serif
- **Body text:** Regular sans-serif (Open Sans, Lato)
- **Important callouts:** All caps, medium weight

### **Icons/Graphics:**
- Use simple line icons or emoji for the homework gap section
- Keep it professional but approachable
- Use checkmarks (✅) and X marks (❌) liberally in the comparison table
- Add subtle background graphics (kitchen equipment silhouettes, ingredient outlines)

### **Layout Tips:**
- Use alternating background colors (white and light seafoam) for sections
- Add thin divider lines between major sections
- Make the comparison table the visual focal point (largest section)
- Use white space generously - don't cram
- Make CTA button/box highly visible with contrasting color

### **Visual Hierarchy:**
1. Main headline (largest)
2. The comparison table (most detailed)
3. "The Bottom Line" callout (second largest)
4. Supporting sections (medium)
5. Footer/CTA (clear but not overwhelming)
