🚴 HIIT BIKE WORKOUTS (7 Total)
HIIT Bike 1: Sprint Ladder
Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Warm-up 3 min easy
  • 30sec sprint → 30sec recovery 1 round
  • 45sec sprint → 45sec recovery 1 round
  • 60sec sprint → 60sec recovery 1 round
  • 75sec sprint → 75sec recovery 1 round
  • Back down ladder 60-45-30sec
  • Cool-down 3 min easy
HIIT Bike 2: Tabata Blaster
Total Time: 16 minutes
  • Warm-up 4 min moderate
  • 20sec all-out → 10sec rest 8 rounds (4 min)
  • Recovery 2 min easy
  • Repeat Tabata set 4 more rounds
  • Cool-down 4 min easy
HIIT Bike 3: Power Pyramids
Total Time: 25 minutes
  • Warm-up 4 min build
  • 15sec max → 45sec easy Repeat 3 rounds
  • 30sec hard → 30sec easy 3 rounds each
  • 45sec mod-hard → 15sec easy Then 60sec steady
  • 2min recovery between sets 3 total sets
  • Cool-down 3 min easy
HIIT Bike 4: Endurance Intervals
Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Warm-up 5 min progressive
  • 3min hard (85%) → 2min recovery 1 round
  • 4min mod-hard (80%) → 2min recovery 1 round
  • 5min steady-hard (75%) → 2min recovery 1 round
  • 3min hard (85%) → 2min recovery 1 round
  • Cool-down 4 min easy
HIIT Bike 5: Speed Play Chaos
Total Time: 18 minutes
  • Warm-up 3 min easy build
  • 90sec hard → 30sec easy 1 round
  • 20sec sprint → 40sec easy 1 round
  • 2min moderate → 60sec recovery 1 round
  • 45sec hard → 15sec easy 1 round
  • 30sec all-out → 90sec recovery 1 round
  • Cool-down 3 min easy
HIIT Bike 6: Threshold Builder
Total Time: 22 minutes
  • Warm-up 4 min gradual
  • 6min threshold (can barely talk) 1 round
  • Recovery 2 min easy
  • 4min threshold 1 round
  • Recovery 90 sec easy
  • 2min threshold → Cool-down 4 min easy
HIIT Bike 7: Fat Burn Finisher
Total Time: 24 minutes
  • Warm-up 3 min easy
  • 2min moderate-hard effort 6 rounds
  • 30sec all-out sprint After each 2min
  • 90sec active recovery Between rounds
  • Cool-down 3 min easy

📋 How to Use This Program

Equipment: Pull-up bar with dip station, foldable bench, stationary bike, dumbbells

Schedule: 4 days per week - Saturdays (Cardio + Abs), Monday/Tuesday/Thursday (Lifting only)

Progression: Weeks 1-2 focus on form, Weeks 3-4 increase intensity, Weeks 5-7 push for results

Rest: 30-60 seconds between sets for cutting, 60-90 seconds for strength moves

New Workouts: Red-bordered lifting workouts are the 6 brand new routines for maximum variety
