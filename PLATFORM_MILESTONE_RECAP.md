## What the Platform Is
A modern workforce-learning platform designed specifically for skilled-trades and hands-on career pathways.

It combines:
- Career-path learning experiences tailored to trades and technical roles.
- Practical, structured training workflows that map to real-world outcomes.
- Institution-ready administration and integration capabilities.
- AI-assisted orchestration to support curriculum operations and learner progression.

---

## The Problem It Solves
Most legacy education systems were built for broad academic delivery, not skills-first pathways tied to employability.

This platform addresses:
- Limited trade-specific tooling in mainstream LMS ecosystems.
- Fragmented curriculum and operational workflows.
- Slow implementation cycles for institutions adopting workforce programs.
- Weak connective tissue between learning systems and employer-relevant outcomes.

---

## Why This Matters Now
The market demand for career-ready talent in trades and technical pathways continues to rise across K-12, community colleges, CTE programs, and workforce partners.

Institutions need solutions that:
- Work inside existing LMS infrastructure.
- Support modern instructional delivery.
- Align learning experiences with workforce realities.
